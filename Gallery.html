Ways you can help prevent Climate Change

Use 100% green power

Humans have been by far the largest source of greenhouse gas emissions caused by human activity because of their immense use of energy. Thus, by fully switching to green power, we can reduce greenhouse emissions greatly.

Save Energy

Saving energy does not only allow you to save more money, but it also helps cut gas emissions, it’s a win-win situation for everyone!

Let your voice be heard

Join public rallies and climate strikes, and show your government, show the world that we should take further action because this crisis is serious and it will not stop if we don’t do anything to stop it.

Get the most of your diet

It’s better to switch to organic when buying fruits and vegetables because not only is it healthier because it contains fewer harmful substances, but it is also good for the environment and the climate.

Avoid using plastic

Since plastic is produced from fossil fuels, it is very dangerous for our climate because it emits greenhouse gases, avoiding plastic usage would create a very huge impact towards our battle against this drastic climate change.

Share

Share the things that you have plenty of, this way, factories won’t have to produce more products, furthermore resulting to less gas emissions.

Lessen your internet usage

Everything we do in the internet causes emission of Carbon Dioxide because every data we’re producing needs energy. Therefore, instead of using the internet all day long just simply go outside and have fun.

Use solar energy

Solar energy is a carbon-free source of energy. Thus, unitilizing solar energy instead of burning fossil fuels would be beneficial to the environment as it would reduce the amount of greenhouse gases in the atmosphere.

Avoid Riding Vehicles

Cars and motorcycles are one of the largest contributors to the total of gas emissions, and walking or using a bike is not only good for the climate but also good for your health because it gives us our daily exercise.

Protect Our Forests and Plant More Trees

This has been widely spread, and yes it is very helpful for our environment because trees absorb Carbon Dioxide and produce oxygen as a by-product of photosynthesis which is very important for our survival, thus planting trees is very convenient because it lessens the carbon dioxide in our atmosphere, and it produces more oxygen.

Think About The Outcomes Of Your Decisions

Consume more carefully, and support those companies that are actually caring for our environment because not all of the companies that we see right now are actually doing anything that would be good for our climate, in fact, those companies are even one of the greatest contributors to the global emissions (approximately 70%)
